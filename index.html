Terraria guide

There are four main classes, Warrior/Melee, Ranger/Range, Sorcerer/Mage And Summoner
Warrior/Melee is the most generic of them all but also the best throughout the playthrough, This is normally everyones first playthrough class, as all weapons and armor are quite simple to get Ranger class tends to stay balanced throughout the entire playthrough, Ranger isnt overpowered towards endgame but he is defiently not weak and you will most likely know this throughout your playthrough
